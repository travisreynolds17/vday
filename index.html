So the other day

My new boss asked if I was married, dating, that sort of thing. So, of course, I told him about you. What's she like, he asked?

Well, I said, she is fierce. If I had a tenth of her strength, her dedication, her stand-up-to-it-ness, I'd be unstoppable. Unflappable. Sir, said I, if I was anything like her, I could not. Be. Flapped.

Also told him you're hot, because I am a purveyor of the truth.

I guess, I told him, the thing I like most about you is that I feel like I could trust anything to be safe with you. Not just little things like watching a pet for a weekend or something. Like, I could trust the future with you. You just kind of are. You's a force of nature, lady.

I love you, Shannon. Sometimes I'm infuriating, I know, but it's because somewhere deep down I'm sure you're out of my league. Happy Valentine's Day, sweetness.

And don't worry, I got you a real card, too. ;)
